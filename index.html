Introducing the all new high quality outdoor inline skates VNLA Parfait. This super cute and super comfortable high top skate made with microfiber uppers in 3 vibrant hues, features outdoor wheels, ABEC 7 bearings and aiuminium plates.

Key feature

The 58mm x 32mm, 78A transparent polyurethane wheels complete with ABEC-7, 8mm bearings are ideal for outdoor skating, whether you are leisurely cruising the sidewalk or grinding at the skate park. You can also enjoy these skates at the rink too!

The Parfait is 20% lighter than most high-top roller skates!

The boot is mounted to an aluminum plate and double-action aluminum trucks with 85-90A polyurethane cushions, finished with a rubber adjustable toe stop.
